Garbage Calzones

Description

This may look like a normal calzone but it's far from it. This disgusting amalgamation is one of france's most famous fuck ups. This hot pocket of hell is filled to the brim with steaming hot garbage. Everything from used tissues to burnt que tips. When it comes out of the oven you can't help but throw up.

Ingredients

  1. pizza dough
  2. garbage of any kind
  3. a fork

Steps

  • thaw pizza dough(if homemade)
  • flaten pizza dough until it reaches the size you wanr
  • pour items from your garbage can onto the pizza dough
  • arange the items in a semi circle on the flattened pizza dough
  • flip the uncovered half of the pizza dough onto the adjacent side over the garbage
  • with a fork Press the edges of the place where the two halfs of pizza dough meet
  • throw it in the oven for a little while
  • voila, se finito
